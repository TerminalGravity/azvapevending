# AZ Vape Vending — Website Sitemap

**Domain:** azvapevending.com (to acquire)

## Purpose

The website exists to:
1. **Generate venue owner leads** — Primary conversion goal
2. **Establish credibility** — Professional presence
3. **Differentiate from competitors** — Modern, trustworthy
4. **SEO capture** — "vape vending Arizona" keywords

- Simple form: Name, Venue, Email, Phone
- Instant confirmation email
- Slack/Discord notification to sales

### Secondary CTAs:
- "Schedule a Call" (Calendly embed)
- "Download Our Pitch Deck" (email gate)
- "See Our Machines" (product page)
